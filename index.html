PokeBall Mania!

Welcome to Pokeball Mania! The directions are simple.

1.) The computer will select a random number between 19 and 120 and display it accordingly.

2.) There will be four pokeballs varying in color and their value may be between 1 and 12.

3.) The user must click on each ball until their score matches that of the computer's.

4.) If the user's score goes over, an alert will pop up stating you lost the game. Click ok and you can play again.

5.) If you match the computer number, an alert will pop up stating you won the game. Click ok and you can play again.

Good luck and happy Clicking!
